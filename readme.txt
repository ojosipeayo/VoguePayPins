Pin Dispenser for Voguepay allows you sell dispensable Product such as card pins and accept payment on your Wordpress blog,page,article  via Visa Card, MasterCard and Verve Card.
Product are delivered through emails.You get notified by Voguepay on each sales and when running out of stock. 

Manage product inventory on Voguepay .[Merchant Product](https://voguepay.com/merchant_product)



## Description ##

> Before you start accepting payment on your site,Sign up and login to your [Voguepay Account](https://voguepay.com/) go to <strong>Menu</strong>, <strong>Business Solutions</strong> then <strong>Product Management</strong>, then <strong>Create product category</strong>  submit. finally copy <strong>Product code</strong>.to use as shortcode on this plugin <strong>It is very important that you do this before you start using this plugin.</strong>

A Simple wordpress payment plugin for selling pins e.g Recharge Cards, Jamb Scratch Cards e.t.c.

Usage: Use a shortcode tag in your wordpress page or articles to add the button on your wordpress page

[voguepay item="scratch card" code="8979770a667"]




Voguepay is a Nigerian Payment Gateway

Voguepay is a unique online payment processor offering a secure and easy-to-use means of transacting business online.

VoguePay allows site owners to recieve payment for their goods and services on their website with zero setup fee.

To signup for Voguepay visit [here](https://voguepay.com)

With this plugin, you will be able to accept the following payment methods selling your product:

* __MasterCard__
* __Visa Card__
* __Verve Card__

= Note =

This plugin is a tunnel to your merchant product store created on voguepay.
